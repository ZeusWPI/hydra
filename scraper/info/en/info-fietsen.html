Fixed locations

Always check the opening hours on the website of the location you want to go to, as exceptional closures are possible.

Central rental location Kattenberg

Students and staff can go here to rent bicycles and for repairs.

Kattenberg 2 (map)
Tel: 09 266 77 00
E-mail: info.defietsambassade@stad.gent
Website: https://fietsambassade.gent.be/en/node/100

Opening hours

  • Monday: 9am – 12pm and 1pm – 4pm
  • Tuesday: 12pm – 4pm
  • Wednesday: 8am – 12pm
  • Thursday: 10am – 12pm and 1pm – 6:45pm (repair shop 1pm – 4:45pm)
  • Friday: closed

The repair shop closes 15 minutes earlier than the rental shop sluit 15 minuten eerder dan de verhuur (except on Thursday afternoons, when it closes at 4:45pm).

Bicycle depot

Here all found bicycles are stored (except the bicycles from the underground parking at Gent-Sint-Pieters). You can also get your bike registered.

Maaltebruggestraat 191 (map)
Tel: 09 266 77 00
E-mail: info.defietsambassade@stad.gent
Website: https://fietsambassade.gent.be/en/node/102

Opening hours

  • Monday: 4pm – 6pm
  • Tuesday: by appointment, 10am – 12pm and 1pm – 4pm (appointment at least one day in advance)
  • Wednesday: 1pm – 4pm
  • Thursday: 4pm – 6pm
  • Friday: 12pm – 3pm

Closed on holidays, during the Ghent Festivities and during the Christmas holidays.

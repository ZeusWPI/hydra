Hydra

With Hydra you're always up to date with the latest news and activities of your favorite clubs. Listen to Urgent.fm, read schamper or decide whether today's menu in the restaurant is to your liking.

Wherever you are, with Hydra you always have Ghent University with you!

Activities

Have you got nothing to do tonight? In our calendar you will surely find something to your liking.

News

Get updates about what happens at Ghent University and your favorite clubs.

Info

Not sure when the University is closes or wondering where you can fix your bike? All those questions and many more are answered in this section.

Urgent.fm

Live-streaming of Urgent.fm: your favorite radio, always with you.

Social media

Follow us on Facebook.

Student Kick-Off

See where and when the bands play, more information about your favorite stand in the Student Village!

Student Restaurant menu

Lost in Ghent and suddenly hungry? Thanks to our map you know where the nearest student restaurant is and what's on the menu.

Schamper

Stay up to date about the latest news with the official and independent student paper from Ghent University.

Open source

Hydra is developed by Zeus WPI, student association Computer Science at Ghent University. We believe in open source and thus make this app open. GitHub. Bijdragen zijn meer dan welkom!
